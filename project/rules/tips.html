• When changing colors you can touch and drag from anywhere on the column. Use long slower drags as opposed to short flicks to select the right color.
  • Single tap a color wheel to advance to the next color, double tap to move the previous
  • Tapping a Clue circle from a previous guess will copy that guess to the current row.
  • If you loan your friend your iPhone to play ColorCipher, don't forget to turn off record statistics so they don't ruin your great score!
